Scrambled Eggs

Description

These yolky eggs are the perfect start to your morning.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Olive oil or butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs and beat them until the yolk and whites are combined.
  2. Add the milk and whisk again until the eggs are a yellow color.
  3. Preheat the pan and add olive oil or butter.
  4. Pour in your eggs and cook them for a few seconds before folding and stirring. Repeat this every few seconds.
  5. Stop when the eggs are mostly set and add salt and pepper.
