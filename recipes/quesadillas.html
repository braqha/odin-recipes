Chicken Quesadillas

Description

These cheesy flour quesadillas are a fantastic dinner option!

Ingredients

  • Diced boneless and skinless chicken breasts
  • Oil
  • Diced bell pepper
  • Diced onion
  • Jalapeno
  • Garlic
  • Taco seasoning
  • Shredded cheese
  • Medium flour tortillas

Steps

  1. Add diced chicken breast to a pan with hot oil and add taco seasoning.
  2. After about 5 minutes, add onions, pepper, and garlic, and cook for another 5 minutes.
  3. Remove ingredients from the pan and add 1/2 teaspoon of oil and place in a flour tortilla.
  4. Sprinkle on shredded cheese and add 1/4 of the chicken and vegetable mixture.
  5. Add more cheese and fold tortilla in half.
  6. Cook until each side is crispy.
  7. Repeat until all 4 quesadillas have been cooked.
